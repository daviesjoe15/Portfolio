Brandboards for secure asset distribution

Often companies require third-party users to upload, view or download media content. Our solution to this is hosted in a lightweight content management tool called a Brandboard. It keeps content secure via authentication and an inactivity timeout period whilst making it easy to discover content through smart search and filter.
