Introduction

I was employed as an intern for 1 year at (AP Diving) a diving equipment company. One of my projects was to redesign their dive computer interface, a wrist-mounted computer that monitors critical aspects of the diver's equipment.

The Problem

I was asked to redesign the existing computer display with a new LCD colour screen because the company was behind in this market area.

The software was to remain the same thus restricting the screen to four stacked 56-pixel bands and with the added constraint that no two colours may touch.

The company is recognised for its world-class equipment however it was common knowledge in the dive community that our computer was outdated.
