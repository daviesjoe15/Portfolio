About

Designing things is what gets me up every morning, well, that and coffee☕️. What motivates me to keep learning and pursuing design is a firm belief in design to do good in the world.

When I was 15, my mum turned deaf and it seemed as though we wouldn't be able to share a normal conversation anymore. Six years later she was given something called a cochlear implant, which enabled her to learn to hear all over again. The device was successful and as a result, had a tremendous impact on my family's lives and she has been able to continue her career as a nurse. I feel we are only just beginning to use design to make a positive impact on peoples lives and I'm excited to be a part it.

When I'm not designing, I tend to keep a balanced lifestyle and spend my spare time surfing, biking, climbing and socializing.
